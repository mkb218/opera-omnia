Opera Omnia

Opera Omnia plays a song with pieces of many other songs.

You have some difficult choices to make.

If you want to hear a song played back using the library, you can upload it (check the "play it back" box), or enter its Echonest ID if you have it.

If you want to add a piece of audio to the library, upload it and check the "add to library" box.
